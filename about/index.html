about us

we're a berlin based surf rock/indie rock band that originally started as a solo project of our singer, thomas. in 2025 we came together as a band and started playing live shows.

now, in 2026, after a small lineup change, a new album is in the works, and we're also looking for new opportunities to play shows! check our stuff on bandcamp or your platform of choice, and if you want to, feel free to support us there :)
